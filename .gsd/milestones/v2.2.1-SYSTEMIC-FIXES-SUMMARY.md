## Lessons Learned
- **Architecture over Code**: Do not attempt to optimize queries (`> startDate`) out of band if the legacy underlying data models (String Dates `MM/DD/YYYY`) fundamentally break computational assumptions. Formulate database migration pipelines first.
- **Immediate Fallback Purges**: Structural assumptions on Fallback security contexts (`process.env.JWT_SECRET`) should never be conditionally omitted to fail-soft, as it exposes the instance explicitly. Fail loud and cleanly.
